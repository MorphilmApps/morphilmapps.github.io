Privacy Policy for MemoryTracker

Effective Date: October 23rd, 2025

Last Updated: October 23rd, 2025

TL;DR - Privacy Summary

Your memory card and storage data never leaves your device (unless you enable iCloud sync). We don't run servers, create accounts, or sell data.

Free version with ads: Google AdMob (not us) collects device identifiers, usage data, and approximate location for ad delivery. This data is not linked to your personal identity. You control tracking through iOS settings.

After purchasing "Premium" (lifetime, monthly, or annual): No more data collection from advertising networks. Your app becomes completely private - all memory card data stays on your device and your optional iCloud backup.

iCloud sync (optional): If you enable iCloud sync in settings, your SD card data, storage locations, and backup logs are encrypted and synced to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. You can disable this anytime.

Introduction

Morphilm Apps ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MemoryTracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. MemoryTracker is designed to keep your memory card and storage data private and secure on your device unless you explicitly give permission.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

MemoryTracker stores the following information locally on your device:

  • SD Card Data: Card names, capacities, brands, models, technical specifications (card type, speed, video capability), backup status, project assignments, and storage location assignments
  • Storage Device Data: Storage location names, brands, models, types (NAS, SSD, HDD, Cloud, etc.), capacities, usage information, connection details, backup relationships, and backup logs
  • Project Organization: Project names, color coding, dates, and organizational preferences
  • App Settings: Your preferences for theme, notifications, language (English/French), reminder settings, and iCloud sync toggle
  • Tutorial Progress: Whether you've completed the app tutorial and seen onboarding tips
  • Optional Photos: Photos of SD cards or storage devices you choose to add for identification purposes
  • Backup Logs: Timestamps and details of backup operations between SD cards and storage devices
Important: This data is stored exclusively on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, this data is also backed up to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. We do not have access to this information.

Information Collected Automatically (Only in Free Version with Ads)

Important: The data described below is collected by Google AdMob (not by us, Morphilm Apps) and only when ads are displayed. Once you purchase "Premium" (lifetime, monthly, or annual), this data collection stops completely.

Google AdMob (Third-Party Advertising Service)

To support our small family business and keep the app free, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version. Google (not Morphilm Apps) may collect and process the following information:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Advertising ID/IDFA)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with ads, ad impressions, clicks
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (not precise GPS location)
  • App Activity: Which features you use and when

This information is used to:

  • Deliver relevant advertisements

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google AdMob Partners: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/9012903

Information We May Collect Through Feedback

When you voluntarily submit feedback through our in-app feedback form, we may collect:

Feedback submissions are processed through Formspree, a third-party form handling service. We use this information only to respond to your inquiries and improve our app. Your email address (if provided) is used solely for responding to your feedback.

Tracking and Personalized Advertising

App Tracking Transparency

MemoryTracker requests your permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites for personalized advertising. This is done through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework.

When you first launch the App, you will see a system prompt asking:

"Allow MemoryTracker to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites?"

If you allow tracking:

  • Google AdMob can use your Advertising ID (IDFA) to show you personalized ads based on your interests
  • Ad performance may be better targeted to your preferences
  • You may see more relevant advertisements

If you decline tracking:

  • You will still see advertisements, but they will be contextual (non-personalized)
  • Your Advertising ID will not be used for cross-app tracking
  • Ad frequency and content will not be customized based on your behavior

Changing Your Tracking Preferences

You can change your tracking preference at any time:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Scroll down and tap MemoryTracker
  3. Toggle Allow Tracking on or off

Alternatively, you can manage tracking for all apps:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap Privacy & Security
  3. Tap Tracking
  4. Toggle Allow Apps to Request to Track on or off

iCloud Sync (Optional)

iCloud sync is completely optional and controlled by you. You can enable or disable it anytime in the app's Settings.

What is iCloud Sync?

MemoryTracker offers optional iCloud synchronization using Apple's CloudKit technology. When enabled, your data is synced across all your Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account.

What Data is Synced?

When you enable iCloud sync, the following data is synced to your personal iCloud account:

  • SD Card Information: All card details, specifications, and metadata
  • Storage Device Information: All storage location details and specifications
  • Backup Logs: History of backup operations between cards and storage devices
  • Photos: Optional photos you've added to cards or storage devices (synced via CloudKit assets)
  • Project Organization: Project names, colors, and dates

How iCloud Sync Works

  • Data is encrypted by Apple's CloudKit
  • Only accessible by you through your Apple ID
  • Automatically syncs across your iPhone, iPad, and other iOS devices
  • Changes made on one device appear on all your devices
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy and iCloud terms

Enabling/Disabling iCloud Sync

You can control iCloud sync at any time:

  1. Open MemoryTracker
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Toggle iCloud Sync on or off

Important Notes:

  • Disabling iCloud sync does not delete your data from your device
  • Your data remains on your device even with iCloud sync disabled
  • iCloud sync uses your iCloud storage quota
  • We (Morphilm Apps) cannot access your iCloud data

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

All of your memory card and storage data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. This includes:

  • SD card information and specifications
  • Storage device information and usage tracking
  • Backup logs and project assignments
  • Custom settings and preferences
  • Optional photos of cards and devices

We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to our servers (we don't operate any servers for this App).

iCloud Backup (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync in the app settings, your memory card and storage data is backed up to your personal iCloud account via CloudKit. This backup is:

  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Only accessible by you through your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy
  • Optional (you can disable iCloud sync in app settings)

Data Retention

Your memory card and storage data remains on your device until you:

  • Delete the App
  • Manually delete cards or storage devices within the App
  • Disable iCloud sync (which stops syncing to cloud, but doesn't delete local data)
