GameTracker for iOS

The ultimate companion for board game enthusiasts. Track sessions, analyze statistics, and never forget a game night.

Overview

GameTracker is designed for board game players who want to keep detailed records of their gaming sessions. Whether you're tracking dice rolls, player scores, or game outcomes, GameTracker provides a comprehensive solution for organizing and analyzing your game data.

Key Features

  • Game Session Tracking: Record every game session with detailed information including date, time, players, and outcomes
  • Dice Roll Statistics: Track and analyze dice rolls with comprehensive statistics and probability analysis
  • Score Management: Keep track of scores across multiple games and players
  • Player Profiles: Create and manage player profiles with individual statistics and histories
  • Custom Game Templates: Set up custom templates for your favorite games for quick session entry
  • Tags & Organization: Organize games with custom tags and categories
  • iCloud Backup: Optional iCloud backup ensures your data is safe and synced across devices
  • Dark Mode Support: Beautiful interface that works in both light and dark modes

Privacy First: Your game data stays on your device. We don't run servers or collect your personal information. The free version shows ads (managed by Google AdMob), but upgrade to remove ads for complete privacy.

Perfect For

  • Players who enjoy analyzing statistics and trends
  • Game night organizers tracking multiple sessions
  • Anyone who wants to remember their gaming experiences
