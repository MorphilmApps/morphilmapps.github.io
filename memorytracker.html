MemoryTracker for iOS

The ultimate solution for organizing SD cards, memory cards, and storage devices. Keep track of what's stored where.

Overview

MemoryTracker is perfect for photographers, videographers, content creators, and anyone who manages multiple SD cards and storage devices. Keep detailed records of your memory cards, track their contents, manage backup locations, and never lose track of your valuable data again.

Key Features

  • SD Card Management: Track card names, capacities, brands, models, and technical specifications (card type, speed, video capability)
  • Storage Device Tracking: Manage NAS, SSD, HDD, cloud storage, and other backup locations
  • Project Organization: Assign cards to projects with color coding and date tracking
  • Backup Logging: Record backup operations between SD cards and storage devices with timestamps
  • Photo Attachments: Add photos of your cards and devices for easy visual identification
  • Storage Location Assignment: Track which storage device contains backups of each SD card
  • Bilingual Support: Available in English and French
  • iCloud Sync: Optional iCloud synchronization keeps your data in sync across all your devices
  • Dark Mode Support: Beautiful interface that adapts to your system preferences

Privacy First: Your storage data stays on your device. We don't run servers or collect your personal information. The free version shows ads (managed by Google AdMob), but upgrade to Premium for complete privacy and ad-free experience.
